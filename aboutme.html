My Journey

I started coding with code blocks at age 5 and started toying around with web dev at 7, quickly finding I was passionate. I started building robots with my best friend in grade 6 and realized I had found my career path.

I also started racing when I was 6 years old and saw go-karts for the first time, which led me to start racing myself. Eventually, I started watching F1 and dreaming of making it to F1, ultimately getting me interested in aerospace engineering.

I've continued my journey through computers, coding, and mechanical engineering, and look forward to seeing where I go next!
